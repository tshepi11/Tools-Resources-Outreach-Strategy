How We Sell PULSE

Outreach strategy.

Every prospect goes through these steps. No shortcuts.

1

Identify the right person

The decision-maker is always HR. Head of HR, HR Director, Chief People Officer, People & Culture Lead. Not the CEO. Not the receptionist. Find the HR person's name and email.

2

Send the cold email

Subject line: "Reaching the Employees Who Never Raise Their Hand." The email is short and deliberately vague. It does not explain the product. It makes them curious enough to look at the one-pager. I will give you the template.

3

They receive the one-pager

The PULSE one-pager is the primary piece. It shows them both channels (desk and deskless), the five pillars, the risk engine, and pricing. If they engage and want more, follow with the new PULSE video. Do not send both at once — one-pager first, video if they respond.

4

Follow up

If no response after 5 business days, follow up once. Short. "Hi [name], just checking if you had a chance to look at the document I sent. Happy to answer any questions." If still no response after another 5 days, move on.

5

Book the meeting

If they respond, book a 30-minute virtual meeting. Walk them through PULSE, answer questions, and understand their situation. Ask: "What does wellness look like at your company right now?"

6

The closing meeting

When they are ready, book the closing meeting. For the first 5 clients, I join this meeting. You lead. I provide founder-level endorsement. After 5 clients, you close on your own.

7

Contract and payment

I send the service agreement. The client signs. The client pays (Net 30). When payment clears, your commission is paid within 7 days. You then become the relationship holder. I handle delivery.

The golden rule:

Do not sell the product. Sell the problem. The HR person does not care about PULSE. They care about the employees they cannot see. Start with their pain, not our product.

Getting Started

Your first week.

This is what you do in the next 7 days.

1
Log into Monday.com
I will send you the login. Explore the board. Understand the stages. Add any prospects you already know.
2
Read the one-pager twice. Watch the video twice.
Know both inside out. The one-pager goes first. The video follows if they engage. If a prospect asks you a question, you need to answer confidently without having to check.
3
Review the prospect list
Go through the companies on Monday. Pick 10 to start with. Research each one: how many employees, who is HR, do they have a wellness programme?
4
Send 5 cold emails
Using the template I give you. Five emails. Five companies. Update Monday for each one.
5
Connect with 10 HR people on LinkedIn
No pitch. No product link. Just connect. Build the network.

The target:

10 outreach emails per week. Every week. Consistency builds a pipeline. Not bursts of energy followed by silence. 10 per week. Update Monday. Follow up. Repeat.
